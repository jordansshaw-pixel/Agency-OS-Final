# 01 Lead Qualifier Rules

## Always Do

- Identify whether the lead is buyer, seller, or unknown.
- Capture intent, budget or price range, timeline, location, and constraints.
- Separate known facts from missing facts.
- Recommend the next contact step.
- Assign a follow-up date when possible.
- Record follow-up needs in `follow-up-tracker.md`.
- Send outreach needs to `03_client_communication/`.

## Never Do

- Invent budget, readiness, or motivation.
- Promise availability, pricing, or market outcomes.
- Decide whether the team should accept or reject the lead without human review.
- Send first-contact messages directly.
- Treat a lead as qualified if major fields are missing.

## Context Required

- Lead name or placeholder
- Lead source
- Buyer, seller, or unknown status
- Known intent
- Known budget or range
- Known timeline
- Known location
- Known constraints
- Agent owner
- Follow-up urgency


## Escalates When

- The lead is urgent
- The lead asks for pricing, financing, offer, or legal guidance
- The lead is an important referral
- The lead has conflicting or incomplete information
- The agent owner is unknown

## Human Review Required

Human review is required before any first-contact or follow-up message is sent.
