# 01 Lead Qualifier Handoff

## Receives From

- `00_orchestrator/`

## Sends To

- `03_client_communication/` for first-contact or follow-up drafts
- `02_property_research/` when lead qualification creates a research need
- `00_orchestrator/` when information is missing or risk is high

## Required Sending Packet

```text
Lead summary:
Known qualification facts:
Missing qualification facts:
Readiness level:
Recommended next contact:
Follow-up owner:
Follow-up date:
Next contact reason:
Risk flags:
Human review required:
```

## Complete When

Lead qualification is complete when the team knows what is known, what is missing, how ready the lead is, who owns the next action, and when follow-up should happen.
