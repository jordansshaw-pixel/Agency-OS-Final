# 03 Client Communication Examples

## Example 1: First Contact Lead Text

Input from `01_lead_qualifier/`:

- Referral buyer
- South Austin
- Budget around $750k
- Missing timeline and must-haves

Draft output:

> Hi [Name], Sarah mentioned you may be starting a search in South Austin. I'd be happy to help. Before we point you toward the right homes, can you send over your ideal timing, must-haves, and whether you're already pre-approved?

Review: agent must review before sending.

## Example 2: Property Research Follow-Up

Input from `02_property_research/`:

- Buyer asked about Cherrywood listing
- Research has supplied facts and open questions
- Source limits noted

Draft output:

> I took a first pass at the listing. A few things look worth discussing: [verified point], [verified point], and [open question]. I want us to confirm [source-limited item] before treating it as a decision point.

Review: agent must review source-sensitive claims before sending.

## Example 3: Inspection Issue

> We are reviewing the inspection items now and want to make sure the repair list is complete before anything is submitted. I am confirming the remaining items today so we can stay ahead of tomorrow's deadline.
